Story

History

When I was a child, I had always viewed the world through a landscape of colour. Through mediums of art, I had always expressed myself through each design I did.

It was when I moved to New Zealand and witnessed its natural beauty that I awakened an impulse to bring the world's natural beauty in my works too.

With the support of my friends and family, I now develop designs with the aim in bringing people together through the art of design.

Goals

Through each design that I make, I aim to seek feedback and critique in each step of the way as a means to always improve myself and grow as an individual.

Despite this, I am always proactive in making firm decisions when leading a project to its goals, valuing resilience and always bouncing back.

Process

Before I begin any project, I have conversations with my stakeholders to get to know them in a level where we can establish great communications. Through discussing the brief together, I can grasp a better idea of the goals that we want, and improving the foundation of the outcome.

When we discuss the project's milestones, I get into the development process while still maintaining good communications with my stakeholders, ensuring that the outcome will be truly fit of purpose.

With agile methodologies, version control, and dev ops maintenance, I am able to organize each step of the project in a way that I can easily improve and change, enabling excellence in future proofing the outcome.

Tools
