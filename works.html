Graphic Design in Advertising

Advertising for a Baking Startup

When I was talking with my friends, one of them mentioned that they recently took employment in a startup family business, and they were looking for designers.

I took this as an opportunity for me to put my skills into practice and take on something new. By asking for more context, I found that it was a starting Donut Company, where their family wanted a designer that was accessible, reliable, skilled, and had good communication experiences, so I took the role.

Using my skills in Design, I created 3D renders in reference to their Donut Designs through Blender, where I animated them using keyframes and also created Posters to help them in starting advertising.

UI/UX in Web Development

Website for a Homestay Company

Using a brief that I was given, I was tasked to create a website for a Homestay Company, where I will add details for their services in a modern, organized site.

As this was the first time I developed a design for a Homestay Company, I challenged myself to develop an outcome that is fit of purpose despite confronting the unfamiliar. Taking inspiration from existing websites and consulting my stakeholders with various design drafts, I ensured that I was able to meet all their goals.

Using Figma and Adobe XD to create my wireframes for the design, alongside using skills in Markup/Styling In HTML/CSS alongside JavaScript, I programmed a website with end user considerations in mind.
